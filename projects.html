PROJ-001 Active Development Flagship

Roller Mill PSD
Optimisation Platform

A Physics-Informed Neural Network (PINN) trained exclusively on plant lab data to optimise particle size distribution in activated carbon production. The model uses particle breakage probability functions as physics constraints — it doesn't just pattern-match, it respects the underlying comminution physics.

The PINN predicts two Rosin-Rammler constants: n (distribution width) and d63 (characteristic particle size), enabling operators to tune mill parameters before adjusting the real plant — collapsing trial-and-error cycles from hours to seconds.
